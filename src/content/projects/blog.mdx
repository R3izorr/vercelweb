A small personal blog experiment built on Next.js and MDX. Used as a
scratchpad for long-form posts that don't fit the CTF write-up repo —
everything from reading notes to half-formed ideas.

It is deliberately kept separate from this portfolio so the portfolio can
stay opinionated about *what it is*: about page, projects, and CTF
write-ups.
